srkr college

Sagi Ramakrishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKR Engineering College), is a co-educational, autonomous private Engineering college. It is one of the oldest seminaries formed in 1980's to provide a better quality technical education to students. The college is located in the country side with a scenic beauty in a 30 acres plot in Chinna-Amiram on Bhimavaram - Juvvalapalem state highway; .
